# CivicFix

---

## Tagline

An AI Copilot for Civic Accountability

---

## Vision Statement

An AI system that takes input, validates, and escalates urban issues before communities are forced to suffer prolonged neglect. 

---

## Product Thesis

An AI system that converts unstructured civic complaints into verified, department-ready action reports.

---

## Simplified Workflow

### User uploads:

- photo,

- location,

- optional voice or text comaplint.

Example:

- pothole,

- broken streetlight,

- garbage overflow,

- damaged roadsign.

### Then AI Agents Handle Everything

#### Agent 1 - Vision Analyst

Uses multimodal AI to:

- classify issue,

- estimate severity,

- detect safety risk,

- generate structured metadata.

Example:

> Large pothole on active road near school.

#### Agent 2 - Civic Mapper

Maps issue to:

- responsible department,

- local authority,

- escalation priority.

#### Agent 3 - Action Generator

Automatically creates:

- formal complaint,

- email draft,

- escalation summary,

- citizen report,

- shareable PDF.

#### Agent 4 - Accountability Tracker

Tracks:

- complaint status,

- unresolved duration,

- identifies repeated reports from nearby locations and merges them into unified civic incidents to avoid spam and amplify accountability pressure.
